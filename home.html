Choosing the Right Crop Rotation Strategy

Crop rotation is a key practice in sustainable agriculture. Explore different crop rotation methods and learn how to implement them on your farm.One important factor to consider when choosing a crop rotation strategy is the nutrient needs of the crops. Different crops have different nutrient requirements, and rotating crops can help to ensure that the soil is not depleted of any one nutrient. For example, a common crop rotation strategy is to alternate between a legume crop, such as soybeans, and a non-legume crop, such as corn. Legumes are able to fix nitrogen from the air, which helps to improve soil fertility.
